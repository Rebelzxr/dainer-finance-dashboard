// HOW TO READ THESE
Each mockup shows the same content — hero, stat strip, two pick cards, three trader cards (Buffett / Burry / Pelosi), two news cards, notes. The shape of the information is identical. The vocabulary is what changes. Click any card to open. Toggle dark/light inside each mockup.

// WHAT TO LOOK FOR
Pay attention to: typography choices, whitespace rhythm, where the eye lands first, how dense vs breathy it feels, whether you want to read further or close the tab. Trust the gut reaction — that's the brand.

// WHAT'S NOT IN ANY OF THEM
No purple. No gradient. No glow/halo. No neon explosions. No emoji. No rounded corners. No matrix green. No anonymous-hoodie hacker aesthetic. No generic SaaS template feel.
