ON THE DESIGN

The broadsheet
language.

TYPE
Playfair masthead, Cormorant body, Plex Mono data.
Playfair Display 900 for the masthead and pick tickers — heavy serif with editorial weight. Cormorant Garamond italic for body and decks. IBM Plex Mono for tables and metadata. No sans-serif body anywhere. Magazine page DNA.
COLOR
Cream paper, ink black, vermillion.
Light is the primary mode — warm newsprint cream #FFFDF6. Dark mode flips to noir gazette. Single bold accent: vermillion #D9382B — the color of red-ink editorial corrections and engraver's blood. Dark mode lifts it brighter for legibility.
RULES + DINGBATS
Sharp 1px rules, ⁂ asterisms.
Hard rules separate every section. Double rules at masthead and footer. Dingbats (⁂) flank section heads. Drop caps on lead paragraphs. Tables use dotted bottom rows. Zero rounded corners, anywhere.
