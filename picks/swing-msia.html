03 · Why Johor

The data-center super-cycle

Four ways to play it. One thesis. Five-plus GW announced through 2030.

Path 1 · Grid toll

TENAGA · monopoly transmission

  • What: Every Johor DC pays TENAGA the grid toll on every kWh. Statutory monopoly. Cannot be disintermediated.
  • Catalyst: RP4 tariff filing locked in; regulated asset base grows with every DC plug-in.
  • Yield: 3.74-3.95% forward. P/E ~16-17x — premium to historical 13x driven by DC narrative.

Path 2 · Own + operate

YTLPOWR · Kulai DC owner

  • What: Vertical bet — owns land, owns power generation, owns DC tenancy. NVIDIA-anchored AI cluster.
  • Catalyst: 500MW → 1.3GW expansion over 5 years. UK water Wessex provides regulated CF stability.
  • Yield: 2.0% (FY25 DPS 8 sen). Forward P/E ~12x. Hold for the capacity ramp, not the yield.

Path 3 · Civils + construction

GAMUDA · DC build cycle

  • What: MY's largest contractor. RM37bn orderbook with 45-50% data-center civils + AU transport + Taiwan diversification.
  • Catalyst: Orderbook target RM50bn end-CY26. MRT3 award pending. Vietnam expansion.
  • Yield: ~1.5% (lower; growth play not income).

Path 4 · Servers + integration

NATGATE · only NVIDIA OEM in SEA

  • What: NationGate is the only NVIDIA Original Equipment Manufacturer partner in SE Asia. Assembles AI server racks for regional DC operators.
  • Catalyst: FY24 revenue +720% YoY. Order book mid-2026 visibility good.
  • Yield: Minimal (growth phase). Highest beta of the four — concentrated single-customer NVDA risk.
04 · MY-specific risks

What kills the ringgit book

RISK 01

Bank Negara OPR cut

NIM compression hits all 3 banks (Maybank, PBBank, CIMB) simultaneously. MY banks = 50% of recommended top-5. Mitigation: TENAGA + PETDAG + Gamuda are rate-neutral.

RISK 02

Government utility interference

TENAGA tariff is political. Subsidies / IPP tariff caps can flip the regulated ROE thesis overnight. Watch every Federal Budget speech in October for utility headlines.

RISK 03

Johor DC slowdown

If hyperscaler capex pauses (correlated with US risk in the US 10-pick), Tenaga + YTLPOWR + Gamuda + NationGate all crater together. 4 of 11 names = ~40% allocation correlated. Diversifier: bank book + IHH + PETDAG.

RISK 04

Acibadem / Turkey FX (IHH)

Lira instability hits ~30% of IHH EBITDA. Re-rating thesis depends on Acibadem normalizing through 2026.

RISK 05

MYR appreciation

Counterintuitive — if MYR strengthens vs USD, EXPORTERS (NATGATE, VITROX) suffer because input/output is USD-priced. Banks neutral. Healthcare mixed.

RISK 06

Liquidity / Bursa retail flight

MY market is retail-heavy. Foreign fund outflow + retail panic = 15-20% drawdowns happen fast. Mitigation: position-size such that 12-month dry powder exists for accumulation in drawdowns.
