HOW TO LEG IN · ALPHA TAX

Execution is
the alpha tax.

Leg-in plan

50 / 25 / 25 across 6 weeks

  • Week 1: Buy 50% notional across all 10 positions at market. Don't optimize entries.
  • Week 3: Buy 25% more. If any name dropped 8%+ since week 1, double that slice.
  • Weeks 5-8: Buy remaining 25% on dips. Being in the trade beats being clever about timing.
  • ETF shortcut: ITA + SMH can both be lump-summed week 1. Diversified by construction.

Rebalance triggers

Quarterly check, annual reset

  • Single position > 18%: trim 25% of excess back into laggards (mostly fires on TSM).
  • Single position drops 25%: if no thesis-break news, add. If catalyst → cut to zero.
  • Annual reset May 2027: re-run the same 5-agent research.
  • Tax lots: long-term gains kick in at month 13.
